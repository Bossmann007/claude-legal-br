## LGPD regulamentação da ANPD

- **Resoluções CD/ANPD:** ANPD has been actively issuing normative resolutions fleshing out LGPD obligations (DPIA/RIPD requirements, international transfer standard contractual clauses, cookies/consent guidance, penalty dosimetry regulation). Check for the latest Resolução CD/ANPD affecting the product's data practices — this area moves quarterly. `[model knowledge — verify current status]`
- **ANPD enforcement actions:** ANPD has opened administrative sanctioning processes and issued fines/warnings against companies for LGPD violations (data breach notification failures, excessive data collection, lack of legal basis). Any specific enforcement action cited should be flagged `[verify]` — do not assert specific case outcomes without confirming against ANPD's public decisions database.

## PL 2338/2023 — Marco Legal da Inteligência Artificial

- **Status:** Brazil's AI framework bill (risk-tiered obligations modeled loosely on the EU AI Act — high-risk AI systems, transparency duties, human oversight) has been moving through Congress. Check current status (Senate approval, Câmara dos Deputados amendments, sanctioning) before relying on any specific obligation or effective date. `[model knowledge — verify current status]`
- If the launch has an AI component and serves Brazilian users, treat PL 2338/2023's risk-tier framework as a directional signal, not a settled legal requirement, until enactment is confirmed.

## CDC dark patterns / padrões de manipulação digital

- **SENACON (Secretaria Nacional do Consumidor):** has been active on "dark patterns" / padrões de manipulação digital in the consumer-protection space — watch for a Nota Técnica or Portaria addressing manipulative UX patterns (deceptive cancellation flows, forced continuity, confirmshaming) under the CDC's general dever de informação (arts. 6º/31) and vedação a práticas abusivas (art. 39) framework. `[model knowledge — verify current status]` — this is a fast-moving area and no single codified "dark patterns" statute exists yet; the current hook is general CDC provisions plus SENACON guidance.
- Auto-renewal / negative-option flows: verify current SENACON/PROCON guidance on cancellation-parity ("as easy to cancel as to sign up") expectations before relying on model knowledge.

## AI transparency and synthetic content

- PL 2338/2023 (see above) would introduce AI-specific transparency and disclosure obligations if enacted — verify status.
- LGPD art. 20 (automated decision-making — right to review of automated decisions affecting the data subject) remains the operative BR provision for automated/AI-driven decisions absent PL 2338/2023 enactment.
- ANPD guidance on AI-generated content and automated decisions: check for recent Resoluções or public consultations. Verify current.
